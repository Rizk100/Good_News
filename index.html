About Nike Air Huarache

Built to fit your foot like a snug wetsuit, the Nike Air Huarache is back.Leather accents on the upper mix with Cordura® fabric, giving a waterproof edge to your street-level favourite.The iconic heel clip and stripped away branding keep the early '90s look you love.

Benefits

  • Cordura® waterproof fabric keeps your feet on rainy days.
  • Originally designed for performance running, the Nike Air cushioning delivers lasting comfort.
  • The plush foam midsole feels soft and springy.
  • The cushioned inner sleeve stretches to your foot for a personalised fit.
  • The rubber sole adds durability and traction
